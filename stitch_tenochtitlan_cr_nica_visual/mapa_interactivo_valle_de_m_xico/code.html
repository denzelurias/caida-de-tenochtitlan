Tenochtitlan

Founded in 1325 on an island in Lake Texcoco, Tenochtitlan grew into the dominant power of the Aztec Empire. At its peak in 1519, it was one of the largest cities in the world, renowned for its complex system of canals, causeways, and monumental architecture.
